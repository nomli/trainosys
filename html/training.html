Training Types

Trainosys-Classroom (Private)

  • Training venue is arranged by Trainosys.
  • Participants are from one organization only. No external participants will be allowed.
  • Course outlines usually can be customized per clients' requests and needs.

Client Premise-Classroom (Private)

  • The training venue is chosen and organized by clients, to save cost and time.
  • Participants are from one organization only. No external participants will be allowed
  • Course outlines usually can be customized per clients' requests and needs.

Remote Training-LED(Private)

  • No logistic costs. Less preparation time.
  • Both the delegates and the trainer will have access to the visual classroom (which is set up on our server) by accessing a web-link during the training.
  • The whole training will be conducted in such a visual classroom. The trainer can correct and guide the delegates on the spot via hands-on practice.
